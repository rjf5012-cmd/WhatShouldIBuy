Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information GiftPicks AI collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

GiftPicks AI does not collect personal identity information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or account data.

We only collect:

  • Information you voluntarily enter into the tool (e.g., “friend,” “birthday,” “tech interests”).
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve performance and reliability.
  • Technical logs required for site security and error detection.
2. How Your Information Is Used

Information you provide is used solely for generating gift suggestions. None of your inputs are used to identify individuals.

  • To generate personalized gift recommendations.
  • To improve site performance and user experience.
  • To ensure the service remains secure and reliable.
3. Cookies

GiftPicks AI may use cookies for analytics and (in the future) advertising. These cookies do not store personal identifying information.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • OpenAI to process AI prompts and generate gift suggestions.
  • Google Analytics (optional) for anonymous traffic insights.
  • Google AdSense (optional) for advertising.

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your data directly with them beyond what is required for functionality.

5. Data Storage

GiftPicks AI does not store personal information entered into the tool. Any temporary processing occurs only in memory or short-term logs.

6. Your Choices
  • You may use the tool without providing any personal information.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.
7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site evolves.
