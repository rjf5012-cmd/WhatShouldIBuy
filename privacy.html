Privacy Policy GiftPicks AI

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

GiftPicks AI is designed to help you generate gift ideas with as little friction as possible. You can use the tool without creating an account or logging in.

This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the site and how that information may be used.

2. Information you provide

When you use the gift form, you may enter details such as:

  • Who the gift is for (e.g., partner, friend, coworker).
  • Approximate age range, occasion, and budget range.
  • High-level interests or preferences (e.g., “tech”, “coffee”, “sports”).
  • Optional free-text notes describing hobbies or preferences.

These details are sent to an AI model to generate gift suggestions. They are used only to create your results and are not intended to be stored as long-term profiles.

Please avoid entering highly sensitive information (for example, full names, addresses, medical details, or anything you would not normally share in a short, casual description of a friend).

3. Technical and usage data

Like most websites, GiftPicks AI may collect basic technical information automatically, such as:

  • IP address and approximate region.
  • Browser type and device information.
  • Pages viewed and basic interaction data.

This information is typically handled by the hosting provider (for example, server logs or built-in analytics) and is used to keep the site reliable, secure, and performant.

4. Use of AI and third-party services

When you submit the gift form, your inputs are packaged into a structured prompt and sent to an AI model provided by a third-party AI service. That service processes your prompt and returns suggested gift ideas.

Third-party AI providers may log prompts and responses as part of operating and improving their services. Their own privacy policies apply to that processing.

5. Cookies and tracking

GiftPicks AI does not require cookies for basic functionality. However, analytics tools, hosting providers, or affiliate programs may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Measure traffic and usage patterns.
  • Attribute affiliate commissions when applicable.

6. Affiliate links

Some outbound links on GiftPicks AI may be affiliate links. If you click a link and make a qualifying purchase, the site may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

When you follow these links, you are subject to the privacy and cookie policies of the destination site (for example, the retailer or marketplace).

7. Data retention

GiftPicks AI is intended to process your inputs transiently—just long enough to generate a response and show it in your browser. Aggregate or anonymized analytics data may be retained for a longer period to help understand and improve site performance.

8. Children’s privacy

GiftPicks AI is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The site is meant to be used by adults who may occasionally describe gifts for children, but children should not be the ones submitting information directly.

9. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time as features change or as hosting and analytics tools are updated. When material changes are made, the “Last updated” date above will be adjusted.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact the site owner using the contact details associated with the domain or any email address listed on the site.
