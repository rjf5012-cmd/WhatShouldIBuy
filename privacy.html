Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GiftPicks AI (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and AI-powered gift suggestion tool.

1. Information we collect

GiftPicks AI is designed to work with minimal personal data. We do not require you to create an account or provide names, phone numbers, or other direct identifiers to use the tool.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Form inputs: When you use the gift finder, you provide details such as recipient type (e.g., friend, family member), approximate age range, interests, occasion, budget, region, and desired gift tone. These inputs are used to generate gift ideas and may be sent to our AI provider for processing.
  • Usage data: We may collect basic analytics data such as pages visited, approximate location (city/country level), device type, and browser type to understand how the site is used.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and, in the future, for advertising (such as Google AdSense). See the “Cookies and tracking technologies” section below.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate gift suggestions based on the context you provide;
  • Improve the quality and reliability of the gift ideas;
  • Monitor site performance and understand general usage patterns;
  • Maintain and improve the website and related services;
  • Support future features such as ads or affiliate links in a way that remains useful and relevant.

3. AI processing

To generate suggestions, we send your form inputs to an AI model provided by a third-party service. That provider may temporarily process and store data to operate and improve their models, in accordance with their own privacy and security practices.

We do not instruct the model to store your data in a way intended to personally identify individuals, and we do not attempt to link form responses back to any specific person.

4. Cookies and tracking technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your consent preferences (for example, cookie banner acceptance);
  • Measure anonymous traffic and usage patterns via analytics tools;
  • Support current or future advertising platforms such as Google AdSense.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, where you can disable or delete them if you prefer. Disabling cookies may affect some aspects of site functionality.

5. Third-party services

We may use third-party providers for:

  • AI processing;
  • Analytics and performance monitoring;
  • Advertising and affiliate links (for example, Amazon or other retailers) in the future.

These providers may have access to limited information necessary to perform their services and will process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data retention

We only retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the site, improve the experience, and meet any legal or compliance obligations. Because the tool focuses on non-identifying context rather than personal identity, data is not stored with the intention of tracking specific individuals.

7. Children’s privacy

GiftPicks AI is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@giftpicksai.com, and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we handle. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You use the site at your own risk and should avoid including sensitive personal information in free-text fields.

9. Advertising and affiliate links

In the future, GiftPicks AI may display ads or include affiliate links to retailers such as Amazon. If you click one of these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Advertising and affiliate relationships will not change our focus on providing useful gift ideas.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or in applicable laws. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date below. Your continued use of the site after any changes means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle data, you can contact us at:

Email: hello@giftpicksai.com
Or via the Contact page.
