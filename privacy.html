Privacy Policy

How GiftPicks AI handles your information

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

GiftPicks AI is designed to be lightweight and simple. We collect only the information needed to operate the site, improve the experience, and understand how the tool is being used.

2. Information you provide

When you use GiftPicks AI, you may enter:

  • High-level details about the recipient (e.g., relationship, age range, interests, budget, region).
  • Free-text descriptions about their hobbies, preferences, or personality.

This information is used to create an AI prompt and generate gift ideas. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not request sensitive personal information such as passwords, government IDs, or payment details.

3. Use of AI services

To generate suggestions, GiftPicks AI sends your inputs to a third-party AI provider. Those inputs may be processed and temporarily stored by that provider in order to generate responses and maintain the quality and security of their services.

Please avoid entering highly sensitive information about yourself or others. Use generic descriptions where possible (e.g., “my friend who likes running and coffee” instead of full names and personal histories).

4. Analytics and log data

GiftPicks AI may use basic analytics and server logs to understand usage patterns and maintain site performance. This can include:

  • Pages visited and actions taken (e.g., form submissions).
  • Approximate location based on IP (e.g., country or region), where supported by analytics tools.
  • Technical data like browser type, device type, and referrer URLs.

This information is used in aggregate to improve the site and troubleshoot issues, not to build individual user profiles.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

GiftPicks AI may use cookies or similar technologies (such as local storage or analytics tags) to:

  • Remember certain preferences.
  • Measure anonymous usage and performance.

You can usually manage cookie settings through your browser. Disabling some cookies may affect the behaviour or performance of the site.

6. Affiliate links

Some outbound links to retailers (for example, Amazon) may be affiliate links. When you click these links and complete a qualifying purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Retailers may use their own cookies or tracking tools when you visit their sites. Their use of data is covered by their own privacy policies, not this one.

7. Data sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, analytics, AI providers).
  • Third parties when required by law, regulation, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

We do not sell personal information to data brokers.

8. Data retention

Data may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the site, analyze usage, maintain security, and meet legal or contractual obligations. Where possible, data is aggregated or anonymized over time.

9. Children’s privacy

GiftPicks AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the site, please contact us so we can review and address the situation.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When changes are made, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the site after changes are posted indicates acceptance of those changes.
