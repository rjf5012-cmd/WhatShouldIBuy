Privacy Policy

How GiftPicks AI handles your information

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

GiftPicks AI is designed to be a lightweight tool for generating gift ideas. We aim to collect and store as little personal information as reasonably possible while still providing useful results.

2. Information you provide

When you use GiftPicks AI, you may enter details such as:

  • Who the gift is for (e.g., partner, friend, coworker).
  • Approximate age range of the recipient.
  • Occasion and budget range.
  • High-level interests or preferences (e.g., “likes coffee,” “into running,” “tech gadgets”).
  • Region or country to help avoid suggestions that don’t ship easily.

This information is used solely to generate gift ideas. You should avoid entering sensitive personal information (such as full names, addresses, or financial details) into the free-text fields.

3. Use of AI services

GiftPicks AI relies on third-party AI services to generate suggestions. Information you submit through the form may be sent to those services in order to create relevant gift ideas. We do not use this information to build our own user profiles, and we do not sell your data.

4. Logs and analytics

Like many websites, we may collect basic technical information automatically, such as:

  • IP address and general location (at a region/city level, not precise address).
  • Browser type and device information.
  • Pages viewed and basic usage patterns.

This information is used in aggregate to understand how the site is being used, diagnose issues, and improve performance.

5. Cookies

GiftPicks AI may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality, performance monitoring, or to integrate third-party services such as analytics or affiliate tracking. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Affiliate links and third-party sites

Some links on GiftPicks AI may be affiliate links. When you click these links, you are taken to third-party websites (such as Amazon). Any data collected on those sites is governed by their own privacy policies, not this one.

7. Data retention

We do not intentionally store the specific gift queries you enter in a way that is tied back to your identity. Technical logs and aggregate analytics may be retained for a limited period for security, troubleshooting, and usage analysis.

8. Children’s privacy

GiftPicks AI is not directed at children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, you can contact us to request deletion of any related data we may hold.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
